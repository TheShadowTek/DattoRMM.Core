TOPIC
    about_DRMMDevice

SHORT DESCRIPTION
    Describes the DRMMDevice class and its methods for managing devices in Datto RMM.

LONG DESCRIPTION
    The DRMMDevice class represents a managed device within Datto RMM. Devices are endpoints (servers, workstations, network devices, etc.) monitored and managed through the Datto RMM platform. 
 
DRMMDevice objects are returned by [Get-RMMDevice](Get-RMMDevice.md) and provide a comprehensive object-oriented interface for device management, monitoring, and automation.
